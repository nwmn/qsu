Mission Statement

Our primary goal is to help foster a thriving queer community at UC Davis. We provide additional resources that contribute to and bring together an already diverse network of LGBTQIA+ organizations on campus through social events, networking opportunities, and participating in local activism. QSU welcomes people of all identities and creeds because developing bonds and friendships is crucial to building an inclusive, robust community.

Community Agreements

"Shared Squared"

We encourage discussion from all members. With that, it's importat to consider how active partcipation can take many forms. Be mindful of how you choose to do so. Try assuming a new role!

Confidentiality

For many reasons, privacy is increasingly a shared concern. We respect that. QSU does not actively identify our members.

"I" Statements

Center your own personal experiences. Avoid generalizations. Consider intersectional perspectives.

"One Diva, One Mic"

Be respectful of others. Don't interrupt.

"Throw Glitter, Not Shade"

Be supporitve. Passive aggressive behavior has no place here.

Practice Self Care

Downtime is essential. Nurture yourself.
